# 02 · Math & Statistics

Not to become a mathematician - to stop debugging models blindly.

## What matters for AI

### Linear Algebra
Data is matrices. Models transform matrices. Without this you won't understand embeddings, transformers or PCA.

Key concepts: vectors, dot product, matrix multiplication, transpose, eigenvalues, SVD.

### Calculus
Neural network training is gradient descent. Gradient descent is applied derivatives. Without understanding the chain rule, backprop is black magic.

Key concepts: derivatives, chain rule, gradients, partial derivatives.

### Probability & Statistics
LLMs generate tokens according to a probability distribution. Evaluation metrics are statistics. Without this you can't interpret results.

Key concepts: conditional probability, Bayes theorem, distributions (normal, binomial), mean, variance, correlation.
